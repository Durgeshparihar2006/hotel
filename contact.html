Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hotel and services.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast is included in most of our room packages. Please check your specific booking details for confirmation.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we offer airport transfer services. Please contact our concierge at least 24 hours in advance to arrange your transfer.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Cancellations made 48 hours before the check-in date are free of charge. Late cancellations may incur a fee equivalent to one night's stay.
